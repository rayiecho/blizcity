The Artist
MOONKHID
MUSIC

Born Omorodion J. Osasenaga, MoonkhidMusic is an emerging Afrobeats and Afropop artist from Benin City, Nigeria. With a passion for creating melodies that began at a young age, his music tells stories of love, life and dreams — inviting audiences into his world.

His debut single Sincere marks the beginning of an exciting journey. With a unique blend of genres and a voice that resonates deeply, MoonkhidMusic is an artist to watch.
