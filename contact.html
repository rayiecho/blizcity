Quick Answers

FAQ

How do I submit my music to BLIZCITY?
Send your demo to demo@blizcity.com with your artist name, genre and a brief bio. We review all submissions and respond within 2–4 weeks.
How can I book BLIZCITY artists for events?
For bookings and live performances, email info@blizcity.com with your event details, date, location and budget. Our team will respond promptly.
Are you open to brand partnerships?
Yes! BLIZCITY partners with brands that align with our values of African excellence, creativity and culture. Reach out via our contact form.
Where can I stream BLIZCITY music?
Our music is available on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Audiomack, Boomplay, TikTok and 40+ other platforms worldwide.
