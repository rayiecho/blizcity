Biography
BORN TO
MAKE MUSIC

Born Omorodion J. Osasenaga on October 8th, MoonkhidMusic is an emerging Afrobeats and Afropop artist from Benin City, Nigeria. His passion for creating melodies began at a young age, shaped by a diverse musical landscape and a deep desire to tell stories through sound.

With a unique blend of genres — Afrobeats, Afropop and elements of contemporary African music — MoonkhidMusic crafts songs that resonate deeply with listeners. His music tells stories of love, life and dreams, inviting audiences into a world built on honesty and emotion.

His debut single Sincere marks the beginning of an exciting journey — a record that showcases his voice, his vision and his commitment to music that matters. As he embarks on this journey, the world is paying attention.
