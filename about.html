Our Story

ABOUTBLIZCITY

"Rooted in Africa. Built for the World."

"We don't just make music — we build culture and amplify voices the world needs to hear."

BLIZCITY is Africa's premier independent music label — founded on the belief that authentic African music deserves a global stage. We are the home of MoonkhidMusic and the Sound of New Era.

From Benin City to the world — BLIZCITY blends Afro Pop, Afro Dancehall and the rich cultural heritage of Africa into a sound that is entirely new yet deeply rooted.

Every track we release is a statement. Every artist we sign is a voice. Every beat we produce is a heartbeat of the continent. This is BLIZCITY.
